The first 30 seconds determine everything. If viewers leave early, YouTube won't recommend your video.

## Why Viewers Leave

Research shows predictable drop-off patterns:
- **20-30% in first 30 seconds:** Value judgment made instantly
- **10-15% at 2-minute mark:** Pacing becomes critical
- **Only engaged viewers reach halfway:** Hook and structure matter

## The Engagement Curve

Typical videos see predictable drops because of:
- Failure to establish immediate value
- Pacing issues (too slow or too fast)
- Lack of clear narrative
- Technical issues (bad audio, visual distractions)

## Actionable Strategies

**Hook immediately:** State core value in first 10 seconds
**Structure for retention:** Place key messages at high-retention points
**Use pattern interrupts:** Visual cuts, music changes every 15-20 seconds
**Deliver payoffs:** Give value every 60-90 seconds

Clean audio and tight editing keep people watching. Filler words and dead air drive them away.
