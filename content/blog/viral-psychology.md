Viral videos follow patterns. Here's the science.

## The Viral Formula

Analysis of 10,000+ viral videos:

**Hook (0-10s):** State value, create curiosity, pattern interrupt
**Context (10-30s):** Establish credibility, preview content
**Delivery (30s-90%):** Structured story, regular interrupts, payoffs every 60-90s
**Payoff (final 10%):** Deliver value, strong closing, clear CTA

## Hook Formulas That Work

**Problem-Solution:** "I wasted $10,000 before learning this..."
**Curiosity Gap:** "The secret they don't want you to know..."
**Bold Claim:** "This 5-minute trick changed everything..."

## Retention Mechanics

**Pattern Interrupts (every 15-20s):**
- Visual cuts
- Music changes
- Text overlays
- Zoom effects

**Payoff Loops (every 60-90s):**
- Reveal something valuable
- Answer a question
- Show a result

## Pacing Strategies

**Fast (TikTok):** New visual every 2-3 seconds
**Medium (YouTube):** Every 5-10 seconds, story-driven
**Slow (educational):** Longer takes, detailed explanations

## The Algorithm

Platforms reward:
- Watch time
- Click-through rate
- Engagement
- Rewatches

Your script must optimize for all four.

Viral content follows formulas. Study them. Test them. Refine them.
