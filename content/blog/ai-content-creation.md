Traditional video editing is dying. AI workflows are taking over.

## The Problem with Manual Editing

Requires:
- Hours of timeline scrubbing
- Frame-by-frame precision
- Technical expertise
- Expensive software

**Result:** 70% of time editing, 30% creating. Backwards.

## What AI Changes

Modern AI can:
- Transcribe audio 99%+ accurate
- Identify filler words automatically
- Detect optimal cut points
- Preserve natural pacing

**Processing time:** 10 minutes for 1-hour video

## The Workflow Shift

**Old:** Film > Import > Scrub > Cut > Color > Audio > Export
**New:** Film > Upload > AI processes > Review > Export

**Time savings:** 80-90%

## What Creators Gain

Instead of 8 hours editing:
- Create more content
- Engage with audience
- Build strategy
- Rest

## The Adoption Curve

- **2024:** AI matches human editors
- **2026:** AI exceeds human speed
- **2028:** Manual editing becomes rare

## Getting Started

Start with:
1. Auto filler removal
2. Auto captions
3. Rough cut suggestions

Then expand to color, audio, pacing.

The shift is inevitable. Adapt now or later?
